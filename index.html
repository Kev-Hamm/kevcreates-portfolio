FAQ

Common buyer questions.

Matched to the Fiverr listing so the portfolio answers objections before a buyer clicks through.

Is this hand-drawn art?

No. This is AI-assisted comic and manga page creation with human planning, curation, editing direction, and visual QA.

Can you copy a specific artist's style?

No. I can work with broad directions like black-and-white manga or cinematic sci-fi comic, but I will not copy a living artist or studio exactly.

Can I use the pages commercially?

Commercial use is available when you provide original characters, licensed references, or references you have permission to use. You are responsible for rights clearance.

Will characters match perfectly in every panel?

AI-assisted comic work can vary between panels. Standard includes stronger continuity review to reduce obvious drift, but exact replication is not guaranteed.

Do you include lettering?

Basic composition guidance is included. Full lettering, speech balloon cleanup, and typography can be added as an extra or custom offer.

What files do I receive?

PNG or JPG final pages by default. Standard includes prompt or production notes and a visual QA report. Source project files are not included unless agreed in a custom offer.

What counts as a revision?

A revision is a scoped adjustment like fixing visible artifacts, improving panel clarity, or adjusting a character detail. New scripts or style directions need a new order or add-on.

Can you make NSFW, celebrity, or copyrighted fan art?

No explicit sexual content, non-consensual likeness work, or exact copyrighted character replication. Original genre-inspired characters are fine.
