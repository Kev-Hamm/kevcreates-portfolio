How It Works

A structured production pipeline — not just random AI image dumps.

1

Brief & Script

Send your script, scene outline, or character descriptions. I'll clarify scope and plan the panels.

2

Panel Planning

Each page gets a structured layout plan — camera angles, character placement, action beats, dialogue flow.

3

Generate & Curate

AI generation produces multiple candidates per panel. I pick the strongest and refine for consistency.

4

Visual QA & Deliver

Every page gets a quality check: character continuity, anatomy, artifacts, and composition — then you get the files plus production notes.
